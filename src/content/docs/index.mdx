## About

I'm Michael Borck, a developer and educator at Curtin University in Perth, Western Australia. I build AI-powered tools that respect privacy, run locally, and put learners first. I lead [LocoLabo](https://locolabo.org), an applied AI research initiative that maps what's possible with small models on modest hardware. When I'm not building or teaching, I'm making indie games at [RetroVerse Studios](https://retroverse.studio) or pointing a telescope at the sky from [The Little Perth Observatory](https://thelittleperthobservatory.com).
